HOLY GUACAMOLE

This is a recipe for the probably the most delicious guacamole you will ever eat (but not really because it isn't real.)

INGREDIENTS

  • Avocado
  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • Garlic
  • Lime juice
  • Paprika
  • Salt and Pepper

STEPS YOU GOTTA TAKE

  1. FIRST YOU GOTTA SMASH THOSE AVOCADOS UP
  2. THEN YOU GOTTA SLICE AND DICE THE ONIONS, TOMATOES, AND GARLIC
  3. NEXT UP YOU GOTTA MIX ALL THAT TOGETHER
  4. THEN MIX IN THAT LIME JUICE, PAPRIKA, AND SALT AND PEPPER TO TASTE
