YES SIR, STIR FRY

This is a recipe for the probably the most delicious stir fry you will ever eat (but not really because it isn't real.)

INGREDIENTS

  • Jasmine Rice
  • Meat of Your Choice
  • Onion
  • Broccoli
  • Garlic
  • Bell Pepper
  • Sesame Oil
  • Soy Sauce
  • Salt and Pepper

STEPS YOU GOTTA TAKE

  1. FIRST YOU GOTTA CUT EVERYTHING UP IN STIR FRY SIZE
  2. THEN YOU GOTTA HEAT UP THAT SESAME OIL
  3. NEXT UP YOU GOTTA SAUTE EVERYTHING IN SMALL BATCHES UNTIL DESIRED TEXTURE
  4. RUN THAT RICE THROUGH THE PRESSURE COOKER - DON'T EVEN TRY TO WORK HARD
  5. ONCE EVERYTHING IS COOKED, SEASON TO TASTE AND EAT UP!
